**Astro Dungeon** is a 2D endless top-down shooter where you play as a stranded astronaut defending against waves of alien enemies. Players navigate procedurally generated arenas, collect power-ups, and aim strategically to survive as long as possible.

**Developer:** Lennart Weissenstein  
**github repository:** [lweissenstein/astro-dungeon](https://github.com/lweissenstein/astro-dungeon)

---

## Context & Scope

Astro Dungeon is a solo university project focused on building a complete endless game from concept to release.

- Designed as an endless arcade shooter to allow rapid iteration
- Emphasis on gameplay feel, responsiveness, and difficulty scaling
- Prioritized mechanics and object interaction over narrative content

I developed **Astro Dungeon** solo, from concept and gameplay design to, shader creation, asset implimentation, balancing, and deployment on itch.io, ensuring smooth performance and polished visuals throughout.

---

## Gameplay

Astro Dungeon is an **endless top-down shooter** that emphasizes movement, precision, and strategy.

- Mouse-aimed shooting and 8-directional astronaut sprite
- Variety of spawning enemies and two types of power-ups
- Simple UI, pause menu, and integrated audio
- Local highscore system to track best performances
